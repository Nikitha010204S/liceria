Frequently Asked Questions




  • How do I place an order?

    To place an order, simply browse our website, add the desired items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. Follow the prompts to enter your shipping information and payment details.

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept major credit cards, including Visa, MasterCard, American Express, and Discover. Additionally, we may offer alternative payment methods such as PayPal or other digital wallets

  • Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

    Unfortunately, once an order is placed, it cannot be modified or canceled. Please double-check your order before confirming the purchase.

  • How can I track my order?

    Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this tracking number on our website or the carrier's website to monitor the status of your delivery.

  • Do you offer assembly services for furniture?

    Assembly services may be available for certain items. Please check the product description or contact our customer service for more information.

  • What if I receive a damaged or defective item?

    We apologize for any inconvenience. Please contact our customer service within 7 days of receiving your order, and we will assist you with the return or replacement process.

  • How can I contact customer support?

    You can reach our customer support team through liceria@gmail.com, +91 11 1111 2900, or by filling out the contact form on our Contact Us page.

  • Are product colors accurate on the website?

    While we strive to display accurate product colors, variations may occur due to monitor settings. Please refer to product descriptions or contact us for additional details.
